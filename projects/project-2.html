Mobile Banking App

Streamlining financial transactions on mobile

Role Mobile Product Designer
Timeline 8 Months
Team 9 People

The Problem

A mid-sized bank launched a mobile app to compete with digital banking leaders. However, the initial release felt cluttered and overwhelming. Users complained about difficulty finding features, transaction confirmation delays, and concerns about security trust. The app had a 3.2 star rating and a 40% uninstall rate within 30 days.

Task analysis revealed that simple actions like "send money" or "check balance" required navigating through multiple screens and taps. Users also felt uncertain about security due to lack of visual feedback during transactions.

Goals & Constraints

Primary Goal

Achieve 4.5+ app store rating and reduce uninstall rate

Secondary Goal

Increase active users by 60% within 6 months

Constraint 1

Strict compliance with banking regulations (PCI-DSS, etc.)

Constraint 2

Support iOS and Android simultaneously

Design Process

Research & Discovery

We conducted 15 user interviews, analyzed 5,000+ app reviews, and performed competitive benchmarking against leading fintechs. Key insights:

  • Users want instant verification that actions succeeded
  • Security visualizations build trust more than disclaimers
  • Customizable home screens increase engagement

Design Principles

We established core design principles for the redesign:

  • Trust through feedback: Every action gets clear, reassuring visual feedback
  • Simplicity first: Hide complexity, expose power when needed
  • Finger-friendly: All interactive elements sized for thumb reach

Design & Testing

Created wireframes and prototypes for iOS and Android. Conducted usability testing with 20 users performing common banking tasks. Key findings led to iterations on transaction flows and security indicators.

Solution

Home Screen Customization

Redesigned the home screen with customizable quick actions and account shortcuts. Users can now set up their most-used features in seconds, creating a personalized experience that encourages repeat usage.

Enhanced Transaction Flows

Streamlined "Send Money" and other key transactions to 3-4 taps maximum. Added contextual security verifications that feel natural rather than obstructive. Real-time balance updates and clear success confirmations.

Visual Security Language

Developed a consistent visual language for security states: lock icons, color changes, micro-interactions, and haptic feedback. Users now understand exactly where they are in a transaction and when their data is protected.

Mobile-First Components

Built a component library optimized for mobile: larger touch targets, thumb-reachable navigation, and platform-appropriate interactions using native iOS and Android patterns.

Impact

The redesigned app became the bank's primary digital channel. Monthly active users grew from 20K to 33K in 6 months. Transaction volume through the app increased 3x, and customer support tickets related to app usability dropped by 75%.

Key Learnings

  • In financial apps, trust and clarity are worth more than cutting-edge design
  • Micro-interactions and haptics matter significantly on mobile
  • Customization and personalization drive engagement in utility apps
  • Platform conventions should be respected, not reimagined
